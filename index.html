Blood Donation Information

Eligibility

Age: 18-65 years
Weight: Minimum 45kg
Hemoglobin: 12.5g/dL minimum
No recent tattoos/piercings
No major surgeries in last 6 months

Process

Registration → Medical Check → Donation → Refreshment
Total Time: 30-45 minutes
Donation Frequency: Every 3 months
Donation Amount: 350-450ml

Benefits

Free health checkup
Reduces risk of heart disease
Saves up to 3 lives
Free refreshments
Certificate of appreciation

Safety Measures

  • Sterilized equipment
  • Trained medical staff
  • Sanitized environment
  • Temperature checks
